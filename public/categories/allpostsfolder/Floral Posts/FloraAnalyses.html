Flora

A guide on incorporating flora into your builds

An analysis of the techniques used in the build "Forest Demo" by patrix from Sketchfab

Note! Model is interactive! RMB to pan, LMB to rotate, Scroll to Zoom
For mobile users, drag with two fingers to pan, drag with one finger to rotate, pinch to zoom



Flora is one of the easiest ways to instantly add a touch of life to a build. It tells stories, it shows growth, it eases your build into the rest of your world, and it adds nuance. This 3D Model, though not perfect, is a good introduction of building realistic-looking vegetation.
