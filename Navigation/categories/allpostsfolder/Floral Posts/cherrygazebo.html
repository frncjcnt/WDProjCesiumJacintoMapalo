Cherry Gazebo

Beginner

❌ No 3D Model

An easy-to-build Sakura Cherry Blossom Themed Gazebo! This build only provides the EXTERNAL design.

Materials Needed

  • Stripped Birch Log × 16
  • Birch Planks × 26
  • Birch Stairs × 3
  • Birch Slabs × 73
  • Birch Trapdoor × 24
  • Birch Fence × 35
  • Chains × 12
  • Lantern × 8
  • Flowering Azalea Leaves × 44
  • Cherry Leaves × 36
  • Spore Blossom × 2
  • Dirt × 114
  • Peony × 18
  • Glow Berries × 8

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Foundation

First, make sure to mark the base foundation of the actual gazebo. Check the measurements shown on the image.

Step 2: Fill in The Foundation

For the foundation outline, use birch planks all around. As for the filling, use dirt, or grass.

Step 3: Add Stripped Birch Log and Fences

On the front, back, and side, add stripped birch logs. Attached to those are the birch fences.

Step 4: Stairs and Pillars

As you can see form the image, the total height of each pillar should be 5. This does not include the foundation outline. On top of each stripped log, add three birch fences and then one stripped log at the very end. Do this on all stripped log.

Step 4: Final Look and add Stairs

This is what the pillars should look like. Additionally, make sure to add the birch stairs right in front.

Step 5: Add Slabs

Now, please look at the image carefully. Add slabs to every inward corner. This applies to all sides. Meaning to say, every-side should look perfectly identical to one another.

Step 5: Add Slabs: Side View

Now, please look at the image carefully. Add slabs to every inward corner. This applies to all sides. Meaning to say, every-side should look perfectly identical to one another.

Step 5: Add Slabs: Back view

Now, please look at the image carefully. Add slabs to every inward corner. This applies to all sides. Meaning to say, every-side should look perfectly identical to one another.

Step 6: Final Look and add Stairs

Now, we move onto the hard part. As for the roof base, use birch slab in the places shown in the picture. Scroll down for the topi view as well.

Step 6: Top View and Added Trapdoor

Now, we move onto the hard part. Except for the entrance part, make sure to add trapdoors as showsn in the image. Just a small tip, if it looks symmetrical and even, then it is correct! All sides MUST be the exact same!

Step 7: Leaves and Slabs

Using the birch slabs, make another layer as shown in the image. Follow the image as to where to put the leaves.

Step 8: Repeat

Repeat that same method as shown on the image.

Step 9: External Roof Design

For this section, use birch trapdoors to line 5 times to the roof external. Make sure to do this on all sides.

Step 9: Leaves

Add the leaves on the corner of the gazebo. Make sure to do this on all sides.

Step 10: Slight Internal Design

Even though this guide does not fully cover the internal design, we still added this section.

Use chains as shown: one chain + lantern on the left, two chains + lantern on the right. Repeat on all sides.

Step 11: More Internal Design

Add Spore Blossoms only to the front and back of the roof. Place glow berries on the roof corners going down the gazebo. Repeat on all sides.

Step 12: External Design Again

Use a mix of leaves and place them around the outside of the gazebo as shown in the image. Make sure all sides are identical.

Step 12: Road

Finally, add the surrounding road design to complete the build.
