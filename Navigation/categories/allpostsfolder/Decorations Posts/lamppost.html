Road Lamp-Post Design

Beginner

❌ No 3D Model

An easy-to-build road lamp-post!

Materials Needed

  • Stone Brick Wall × 1
  • Spruce Fences× 4
  • Spruce Trapdoor × 2
  • Lantern × 1

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Foundation

First, simply follow the image to create the base of the lamp-post. Use the stone brick wall for the main post structure. Then, place three fencess up.
Fun Fact: The first sources of artificial light date back more than 70,000 years

Step 2: Trap Door & Fence

Next, place one fence facing outward. On top of the fences, add the two spruce trapdoors as the image shows.

Step 3: Final Step: Add Lantern!

Of course, what would a lamp-post be without a lamp? Finally, place the lamp below the fence as shown in the image. And you're done!
