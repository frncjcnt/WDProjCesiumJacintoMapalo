Simplistic Desk Design

Beginner

❌ No 3D Model

An easy-to-build simplistic desk design!

Materials Needed

  • Spruce Trapdoors × 9
  • Spruce Stair × 1
  • Book Shelves × 2
  • Barrel × 2
  • Book Shelves × 2
  • Item Frame × 2
  • Lantern × 1
  • Book Shelves × 2
  • Lectern × 1
  • Book Shelves × 2
  • Flower Pot × 1
  • Books × 5, you may add what more or less
  • Feather × 1
  • Oak Sapling × 1
  • Spruce Sign × 3

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Foundation

First, simply follow the image to create the base of the simple desk.

Step 2: Add Lectern and Barrel

Next, place a lectern in the middle of the best, and add a barrel on either side of the desk as shown in the image.

Step 3: Add Lantern, Bookshelf, Item Frame, and Pot!

Put the bookshelves on the top side on either side of the inside frame. Hang the lantern from the middle most top. Add the item frames as shown in the image.

Final Step: Small Details

Put some books on the shelves -- you do not need to fill it up. After, place the sapling inside of the pot, and add the items inside the item frame as shown in the image. Lastly, add the spruce stair facing right in front of the desk, and add three signes surrounding it to replicate the armrests of a chair. Don't forget to add a book and quill on the lectern!
