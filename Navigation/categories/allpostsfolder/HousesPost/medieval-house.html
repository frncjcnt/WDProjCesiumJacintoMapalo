Medieval Survival House

Beginner – Intermediate Build Guide

❌ No 3D Model

A cozy medieval-style survival house suitable for early to mid-game worlds. This guide walks you through the step-by-step process on the EXTERNAL design and structure.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Foundation

First, lay out the foundation. As you can see from the image, use a variety of stone to create texture.
Tip: Mixing the stone makes it more authentic to medieval builds!
Fun Fact: Many medieval castles had secret tunnels and hidden rooms designed for escape or surprise attacks!

Step 2: Walls

Build oak log pillars on corners, then fill with planks.

Step 3: Roof

Use stair blocks to create a steep medieval roof.
